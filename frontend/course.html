Phase 1: Fundamentals of Web Development

In this phase, you will learn how to design and build the User Interface of a website using HTML5 and CSS3. You will also learn how to use Git for team projects.

  • 2 Languages learned
  • 1 web tool mastered
  • 3 projects completed
  • 12+ hours of live training

Phase 2: Frameworks and Scripting (Internship starts)

You get your hands deep into the Bootstrap framework to improve your web development speed and productivity. An end to end training on JavaScript will be covered to improve the functionality of the website.

  • 3 technologies learned
  • 3 projects completed
  • You start to form your business ideas
  • 30+ hours of live class training

Phase 3: Website optimization and Analytics

You will learn how to optimize your website and improve search ranking using Search Engine Optimization techniques. You will also learn about Analytics Integration to improve website performance.

  • 10+ hours of class
  • Practical experienced gained
  • Set your targets

Phase 4: Final project & Mentoring

You will take the whole ownership of the client project assigned to you. Work very closely with our Tech team to get industry exposure.

  • 1 client project completed
  • Internship certificate received
  • Started making corporate network

Some of the most frequently asked questions

I do know coding at all, can I Register for this Program?
Yes, Even if you do not know even the basics of coding, you can register for this Program. We will be training you from complete basics
Is this program open for all?
Yes, this program is open for all students irrespective of their department and semester.
When will I get my Certificate?
You will get your Internship certificate after successfully completing the final project. In case you choose to quit the program in the middle, you will get a course completion certificate.
What if I want to quit the program in the middle?
We do understand your personal priorities, so you can choose to quit the program at any time. Kindly note, on quitting the program, you might not be eligible for a refund.
At What time will the class be scheduled?
All the classes are scheduled to happen outside the college hours.
What if I have some doubt during the course?
Each student will be assigned a mentor. The student can get in touch with the mentor via Whatsapp, Email, Phone call. The mentor will make sure that your doubts are cleared.
What if I miss a live class?
You will be provided with the link to the session recording. In case you miss the class, you can refer to that recording. For further doubts, you can connect with your mentor.
Will we have class everyday?
You will have a live session every day except Saturday and Sunday.
