What is Skill Safari Affiliate program?

Skill Safari affiliate program is a way where you can offer the Skill Safari services to your customers and get incentivize for the same.

Who can become a Skill Safari Reseller?

An individual or an organisation, who is capable of onboarding customers (students) for Skill Safari services.

How do I become a Skill Safari Reseller and refer a Client?

Visit our website and complete the Partner Signup on https://skillsafari.in/affiliate/ . Once the signup is complete you will get access to the Partner Dashboard.

Even if I haven’t registered my company yet, Can I sign up as a Skill Safari Reseller?

Yes. Skill Safari affiliate program is offered to both individuals and well as Organisations who want to be part of our program.

What documents will be required to register as a Skill Safari Partner?

For registration and commission payouts we would require your company’s/individual’s name, PAN details, address, bank a/c details, scanned copy of cancelled cheque, and the personal details viz. Name, Email, PAN, and Address.

How to track the status of my clients/customers?

You can check the status of your referrals under the your affiliate dashboard

What will be the incentive cycle of the commission payout?

For affiliate Program, Only post which payouts will be done. Commission payout amount, We will raise an invoice for induviduals or if you are a company, raise an invoice and share it with the Support team. Once we receive and confirm the same. Payout will be done in 30 days.
