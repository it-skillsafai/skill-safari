What is Data Science?

According to The Economic Times-2021, nearly 85 percent of companies in India were spirited and undisturbed by the pandemic, owing to the data driven strategies. Every business is driven by data, their patterns and imply them to derive insights and make appropriate decisions. It is the responsibility of a data analyst, machine learning engineer, data science architect or a data scientist to assist the process of problem solving from valuable data. In this intensive and immersive 6 months’ data science certification programme, you will learn to apply your skills to perform analysis and recommend data-driven decisions in any business or domain.

Who is a Data Scientist??
Data scientists are analytical experts who utilize their skills in both technology and social science to find trends and manage data.
Why companies hire a Data Scientists/Analysts?
Data Scientists/Analysts enable business leaders/executives to make data driven decisions. These decisions are among the key things required to run and grow a business.
What are the skill sets of a Data Scientist?
  • Statistics.
  • At least one programming language – R/ Python.
  • Data Extraction, Transformation, and Loading.
  • Data Wrangling and Data Exploration.
  • Machine Learning Algorithms.
  • Advanced Machine Learning (Deep Learning)
  • Big Data Processing Frameworks.
  • Data Visualization.
How Data Science differs from Big Data and Data Analytics?
Big Data hold humongous volumes of structured and unstructured data, either raw or processed information. Analytics organizes the tasks associated with conversions of insights into situations. Whereas, Data Science comprises of tools, algorithms and various options for gaining meaningful insights.
