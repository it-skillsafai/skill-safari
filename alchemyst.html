"ALCHEMYST" name is inspired by the famous book of Paulo Cohelo called "Alchemist", where it says how true perseverance and hardwork can make a person achieve Alchemy(transmutation of the base metals into gold). We will turn a naïve talent into a skilled talent with this program thus the name Alchemyst is chosen. This can be inculcated with good quality training and mentored practice.
