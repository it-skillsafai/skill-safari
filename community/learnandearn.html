How this community will add value to your skills

Get updates on placement opportunities

Get regular updates on different recruitment drives and job opportunities from major MNC and startups from across the country. We will give you tips to crack these companies.

You choose what you want to learn

Each student will have different career goals and dreams. So you can independently learn what interests you the most and master that skill.

Attend live instructor-led sessions

All sessions are live and interactive. Daily and weekly sessions from a wide range of topics handled by our SMEs and Industry experts. 800+ students from 10+ universities attend our daily sessions.

Collaborate with other students to build awesome products

Work on multiple interesting projects by teaming up with like-minded individuals from various other colleges. You might even start your startup one day.

Weekly gamified assignments

There is nothing like having fun while doing your assignments. Each assignment is scientifically designed to bring out the champions inside you.

Compete with students from across the country

Competition is not only there in your college, but also from all the other colleges across the country. These competitions will bring out the best skills in you to excel.

Take part in national-level events and Hackathon

National level events and hackathons will be organised to keep you challenged and brush up on your skills. Win rewards of up to 20K per event.
