Train and Hire models

Choose the right train and hire model for your organization

Model 1
We hire and we train

In this model, we (Skill Safari) will take complete ownership of hiring and training suitable candidates for your organization. The organization can then perform a screening round before onboarding the candidates into the organization.

Model 2
You hire and We train

In this model, the organization will hire the candidates and Skill Safari will take ownership to train the candidates. Based on the roles and responsibilities of the candidates, Skill Safari will develop a customized training model to provide maximum efficiency.
